# TLM Research

**Open project exploring temporal market design for blockchain protocols, with Ethereum as the initial research platform.**

---

## Mission

TLM Research advances the study of **Temporal Liquidity**—the economic flexibility of demand with respect to execution time—and investigates how decentralized protocols can better represent, communicate, and coordinate temporal preferences while preserving market efficiency and decentralized principles.

## Current Focus

* Temporal Liquidity Market (TLM)
* Ethereum fee markets (EIP-1559)
* Proposer-Builder Separation (PBS & ePBS)
* Protocol economics
* Mechanism design
* Distributed systems

## Current Status

**Stage:** Research Framework

The project is currently focused on establishing the conceptual foundations of Temporal Liquidity, reviewing related work in Ethereum protocol design, and developing an open framework for temporal market design in blockchain protocols.

We welcome thoughtful discussion, constructive feedback, and collaboration from researchers, protocol engineers, and the broader blockchain community.
