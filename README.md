# TLM Research

**Open project exploring temporal market design for blockchain protocols, with Ethereum as the initial platform.**

## Mission

TLM Research advances the study of **temporal market design**—how decentralized protocols can better represent, communicate, and coordinate users' temporal preferences while preserving market efficiency and decentralized principles.

## Current Focus

* Temporal Liquidity Market (TLM)
* Ethereum fee markets (EIP-1559)
* Proposer-Builder Separation (PBS & ePBS)
* Protocol economics
* Mechanism design
* Distributed systems

## Repositories

### TLM

The primary repository containing the project's vision, foundation, architecture, research notes, literature reviews, and future mechanism proposals.

## Current Status

**Stage:** Research Framework

The project is currently focused on establishing conceptual foundations, reviewing related work, and developing an open framework for temporal market design in blockchain protocols.

We welcome thoughtful discussion, constructive feedback, and collaboration from researchers, protocol engineers, and the broader blockchain community.
